## Overview

Stellar's SEP-12 defines the standard for uploading and verifying KYC (Know Your Customer) information. When a user wants to deposit or withdraw fiat, the anchor must verify their identity.

## The KycProvider Seam

In NordStern, KYC is implemented behind a swappable adapter called `KycProvider`. 

**Current MVP Status:** ⚠️ **Mocked**
Currently, the customer callback always returns `ACCEPTED`. 

**Future State:**
The real provider (e.g., HyperVerge or Signzy) is a pluggable seam. When implementing a real provider, we implement the `KycProvider` interface rather than scattering vendor SDK calls through our core flow logic.

### Open Legal Questions
> [!WARNING]
> - **Who is the controller of KYC data?**
> - Can it be shared across anchors ("verify once")?
> - What are the consent/retention limits in India?

We do not build a "shared-KYC network" or a KYC data warehouse for the MVP. We store the absolute minimum required data until the legal models are fully defined.
