## Disclaimer

**We do not give legal advice, and neither should the code or docs.**

The legal model for NordStern is deliberately *not finalized*. We architect for optionality. Do not resolve the following open questions in code; they require qualified Indian fintech/regulatory counsel.

## 1. Fiat Holding & Licensing

- **Who holds the fiat, and under what license?** 
  Is it a master escrow/nodal account run by NordStern? Does each anchor hold its own account? Or is it a licensed managed-account model? This changes who the regulated entity is.

## 2. VDA / VDASP Classification & FIU-IND

- Fiat-to-token on/off-ramps may be classified as Virtual Digital Asset Service Providers requiring **FIU-IND registration** (PMLA reporting). 
- **Which entity registers?** Does NordStern register, or does the individual anchor?

## 3. Custody Model

- Are we ever in custody of customer funds or keys? 
- Custody triggers heavier obligations. The MVP avoids custody unless/until counsel approves a specific model.

## 4. Banking Partnership Shape

- Embedded banking vs. bring-your-own-bank vs. BaaS (e.g., virtual accounts).
- Bank risk systems often freeze crypto-adjacent, high-velocity accounts lacking FIU registration, posing a real operational risk.

## 5. Whose License Enables What

- Payments, PPI/wallet, cross-border/remittance, and VDA rules may each apply depending on the specific flows an anchor decides to offer.

When code *must* assume something to proceed, make the assumption a **named, swappable config/adapter** and note it.
